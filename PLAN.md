# CollectARble — AR Trading Card Experience


## Features

- **Scan a card** — Point your camera at a trading card and the app recognizes it instantly
- **3D character appears** — A unique AI-generated 3D creature materializes on the table in augmented reality, anchored to the card
- **Idle animation** — The character gently floats and rotates in place, looking alive
- **Attack button** — Tap "Attack" to trigger a dramatic particle burst effect (energy sparks flying outward) with haptic feedback
- **3 unique creatures** — Three different cards each summon a different character with distinct colors and personalities
- **AR coaching** — Guided instructions help you find a surface and position your card correctly
- **Collection of 3 demo cards** — Since we're using AR image detection, the app will recognize 3 pre-registered card images

## Design

- **Light & clean Apple-style theme** — White and light gray backgrounds with generous spacing
- **Electric blue accent color** — Used for buttons, highlights, and interactive elements
- **Home screen** — A bold hero area with the app name, a tagline ("Scan a card. Bring it to life."), and a large "Scan Card" button
- **AR screen** — Full-screen camera view with a translucent bottom bar containing the "Attack" button (appears after a character spawns)
- **Attack effect** — Bright particle burst radiating outward from the character with a satisfying haptic pulse
- **Smooth transitions** — Spring animations between screens, bounce effect when character appears
- **SF Symbols** throughout — camera icon, sparkle effects, bolt for attack

## Screens

1. **Home Screen** — App logo/title at top, brief tagline, a prominent "Scan Card" button in electric blue, and a small info section explaining how it works (3 simple steps with icons)
2. **AR Experience Screen** — Full-screen AR camera view. Shows coaching overlay until a surface is found. When a card is detected, the 3D character spawns with a bounce-in animation. An "Attack" button slides up from the bottom. Tapping it triggers the particle burst + haptic feedback. A small "X" button in the corner to return home.

## 3D Characters

- Three AI-generated 3D creature models will be created for the demo
- Each creature will have a distinct look (e.g., a fire elemental, an ice guardian, a nature spirit)
- Characters appear anchored to the detected card image in AR

## App Icon

- A clean icon with a light background, featuring a stylized AR card with a glowing blue creature silhouette emerging from it — conveying the "bring cards to life" concept
